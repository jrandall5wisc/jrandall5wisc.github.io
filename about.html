About Jamie Randall

Welcome viewers! My name is Jamie Randall and I am a Sophomore here at UW-Madison majoring in Journalism with certificates in Business and Digital Studies. I am involved in many organizations across campus and one of my favorites is being a staff writer for Moda Magazine. I have written a variety of pieces for this publication and am very excited to show them for you all to read! To learn more about Moda Magazine go to modamadison.com where you will see more amazing writing done by UW students!

One of my hobbies is photography and I have been able to take some amazing photos of my friends and surroundings here at UW-Madison. Enjoy!
